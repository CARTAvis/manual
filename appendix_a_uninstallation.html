Appendix A: Uninstallation¶

We feel sorry to hear that you are leaving CARTA. The following sections are the procedures to fully remove CARTA in your system.

macOS¶

  1. remove CARTA.app at /Application (if CARTA is installed at default directory) or at your customized directory.
  2. remove the directories CARTA and .cartavis at your home directory.

Linux¶

  1. remove CARTA-0.9r-centos folder.
  2. remove the directories CARTA and .cartavis at your home directory.
